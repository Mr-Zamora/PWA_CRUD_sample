A crispy, golden fried spring roll filled with seasoned ground pork and vegetables, often served with sweet and sour dipping sauce.

A comforting beef shank soup with bone marrow, corn, cabbage, and potatoes.

Pancit Canton

Special Occasion

Kaldereta

A hearty beef stew made with tomatoes, liver spread, bell peppers, and potatoes.

Street Food

Balut

A fertilised duck egg with a developing embryo, commonly sold as street food and considered a delicacy.

A refreshing dessert made with shaved ice, sweetened fruits, jellies, beans, and topped with ube and leche flan.

Bibingka

A type of rice cake traditionally cooked in clay pots, topped with salted egg and cheese, often served during Christmas.

Leche Flan

A creamy custard dessert made with egg yolks and milk, topped with caramelised sugar.
