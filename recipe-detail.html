Lumpia

A crispy, golden fried spring roll filled with seasoned ground pork and vegetables, often served with sweet and sour dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup cabbage, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 package lumpia wrappers
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground pork, carrots, onions, cabbage, and garlic. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Place about 2 tablespoons of the filling on a lumpia wrapper. Fold in the sides, then roll tightly to enclose the filling.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Fry lumpia until golden brown and crisp, about 3-5 minutes per side.
  4. Drain on paper towels and serve with sweet and sour dipping sauce.
Prep: 30 min Cook: 15 min Difficulty: Medium
